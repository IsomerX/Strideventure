History of success
Vadim Shushkov

Vadim Shushkov. Operations Director at ACCENT ASSET MANAGEMENT LLC

“Before I joined Accent Capital in December 2017, I had worked in the collective investments market for more than 10 years, mostly in mandatory non-governmental control of financial organizations. Accent Capital represented by ACCENT ASSET MANAGEMENT LLC entered this segment of the national macroeconomic market as an independent fully functional player entered this segment of the national macroeconomic market in the second half of 2017, as soon as it obtained a license from the Bank of Russia for the management of mutual investment funds and joined a leading self-regulatory association of non-credit financial organizations. Therefore, when I started working for Accent Capital, the financial market was a new business for the group of companies.

I was looking forward to working together with Accent Capital, because I saw the opportunity (a) to be involved in building the business processes of this new mutual funds’ management from scratch, leveraging best practices in this segment, (b) to apply my expertise to the fullest extent, as well as (c) to work as a team with recognized professionals and experts.

I started working for Accent Capital as an internal controller of ACCENT ASSET MANAGEMENT LLC. Every company needs such a specialist and, as it evolves, a separate structural unit to ensure compliance with all applicable regulatory requirements established by law and by the Bank of Russia. Ensuring internal control is an integral and demanding task and function of any company involved in financial services. Along with the specific practical duties assigned to a controller working for a financial organization, I was also responsible for the general coordination of the employees and subdivisions of our group of companies in terms of effective integration in the environment of the holding company and further operations of ACCENT ASSET MANAGEMENT LLC, creation and management of investment funds. As I acquired the necessary experience I was organically promoted to Operations Director in the middle of 2019, which was a new position for me. I organized and supported the company’s daily operations in managing close-end mutual funds. So, my promotion at Accent Capital was more of a horizontal move, requiring new competences, additional skills  and new and areas of responsibility.

An Internal Controller does not perform executive functions, his duty is to ensure continuous supervision, independent verification, system analysis and, of course, overall active expert involvement in ensuring the proper fulfillment of this function, creating a framework and opportunities for the company and its employees to fully comply with the established and applicable rules and regulations. While an Pperations Manager is a direct executor that is in charge of organizing and implementing proper business processes, taking into account the opinions of the internal controller and other colleagues within their respective competences and authorities.

ACCENT ASSET MANAGEMENT LLC is a relatively young yet dynamic company in our group. Some projects and tasks have been successfully implemented in recent years. One of the future challenges will be to to preserve the accumulated capacity and multiply it, to implement new plans and ideas. I express my gratitude to Accent Capital for giving me this opportunity to take up and succeed in such a new and important area as operations management!

Info:

Vadim Shushkov is Head of Operations Management at ACCENT ASSET MANAGEMENT LLC. He is responsible for the organization and support of the company’s daily operations in managing close-end mutual funds. Previously, Vadim held managerial positions at management companies and specialized depositories of VTB Group, OLMA, IMAC, etc., and worked at the Russian Pension Fund. Vadim Shushkov graduated with honors from the State University of Management (Moscow) as an Economist specializing in Finance and Credit, holds qualification certificates in various types of professional activities of financial market infrastructure. Vadim is the author of a number of academic and research publications focusing on pension benefits and capital markets.
