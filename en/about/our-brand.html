Accent

True knowledge always wins

It cannot be substituted and cannot be hidden - sooner or later it will be obvious to the person you share it with. It gives you depth - you see more than others. You see imperceptible connections and the hidden causes of events. This cannot be copied or faked - it is your true value.

Do not chase quick gratification,
but achieve
a small but constant
result every day.
Do not take shortcuts,
but choose a long road
that leads exactly to your goal.

True knowledge takes time and perseverance

It is impossible to get the best results without effort. It will rather require much more from us than we expected. That is why so many people stop half-way. To make sure that we get to the end, we have boundless enthusiasm and iron discipline.

Do not try to impress,

but invest time and labor

in true understanding.

Without a deep understanding, what is happening around seems chaotic and random; the causes of events are unclear, and the consequences are unpredictable. It is impossible to know everything perfectly. But by focusing on one thing and making the right effort, we can make it work.

True knowledge is always focused

Do not seek praise,

but stick to

your personal standards.

True knowledge attracts...

People will be grateful to those who helped them to look forward to tomorrow. Having share knowledge and help, you will find loyal friends, partners or customers. Knowledge is worth sharing - you have a tool to make people's lives fuller and their future more secure. Competence breeds confidence.

Do not stop

at "good enough",

do more

than is expected of you.
