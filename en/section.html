Portfolio Diversification

Capital distribution between different asset classes with low correlation between them is the basis for balancing and reducing portfolio risks. The capital invested in commercial real estate allows us to save it in situations when other asset classes decrease in value.

Risk and yield between stocks and bonds

Commercial real estate combines the advantages of two different types of instruments: bonds (real estate brings regular projected income), and shares (real estate gives additional profit as its value grows).

Capital protection

Investments in quality commercial real estate provide a significant level of protection of the invested capital, as they are secured with real property.

Protection against inflation

Regular indexation of rental rates for inflation is common business practice in the segment of institutional commercial real estate. This provides a built-in protection for investments against inflation.

Protection against volatility

Long-term and often indissoluble lease agreements with major companies are standard for investment-quality real estate. This protects such real estate from dramatic value fluctuations, also in difficult economic conditions.
