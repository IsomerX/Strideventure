Portfolio

Sokolniki
Shopping Mall

This contemporary three-storey community format shopping mall is located on a busy road- in the vicinity of the Sokolniki metro station and the iconic park of the same name. This location ensures a diverse demand from locals and visitors to the area for well-known food, cosmetics, clothing and jewelry brands. The facility has an exceptional potential for development through introduction of modern restaurant and entertainment concepts.

Location

The premium location next to the Sokolniki metro station ensures foot traffic created by of local residents and visitors of Sokolniki Park. This key factor will guarantee strong demand for the site's areas from tenants. Besides, additional traffic comes from Rusakovskaya Street connecting the center of Moscow and the residential districts of the Eastern Administrative Area. The future Stromynka metro station will add to the facility’s potential.
