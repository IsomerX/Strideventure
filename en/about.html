Business ethics and transparency

Accent Capital is committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity and corporate governance practices in order to improve its daily operations and to build confidence in our management system. The Company promotes its business in an open, honest and ethical manner and recognizes the importance of protecting its human, financial, physical, informational, social, environmental and reputational assets. Environmental, health and safety policies are in place and communicated to all employees through a Personnel Manual.

Stakeholder relations

Accent Capital engages stakeholders in cooperation based on mutual honesty and respect. The Company seeks a timely and meaningful dialogue with all stakeholders, including shareholders, customers and employees, regulators and other market players.

Relationships with employees

Accent Capital guarantees fair treatment of all employees, taking into account their goals and aspirations. The Company practices equal treatment of all, regardless of their age, race, gender and religion, in accordance with the laws of the Russian Federation. Discrimination, intimidation or harassment is an unacceptable practice for us.

Human rights

The Company recognizes human rights as an absolute value and is willing to work with various structures and institutions to support human rights in our sphere of influence. We do not tolerate or encourage human rights violations. The Company will always seek to build trust, ensure mutual benefit and demonstrate respect for the cultures, customs and values of all individuals and groups.

Investment Community

The Company actively develops relations with the investment community in Russia and around the world; it integrates community investment considerations into decision-making and business practice.
