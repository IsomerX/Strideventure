1

Fund / Capital Management

Our investors receive the following collective or individual investment management services:

1

Strategy and Business Plan
  • Investment strategy - development of all parameters of capital investment, strategy implementation, control and update.
  • Business plan preparation and implementation both within the investment process and at other stages of the fund's/other facility’s life.
  • Market research to assess current situation and adjust strategy.
     

2

Funding and structuring
  • Investment financing, including debt arrangement of any type, compliance and covenants control.
  • Investment, ownership and tax structuring.
     

3

Investment Assessment and Audit
  • Investment assessment - regular identification of net asset value (NAV) of capital/fund based on fair value method (IFRS) according to internal accounting data.
  • External audit, including bidding process and audit arrangement.
     

4

Organization
  • Management and building management processes for making internal decisions and getting third-party approvals.
  • Regulatory compliance: ensuring compliance with capital raising and investment regulations.
  • Organizing the election and activities of the board of directors and its committees.
     

5

Investor Relations
  • Investor relations, including capital raising, notification and meetings, reporting, and getting approvals for the manager’s decisions.

6

Management
  • Leadership over all subordinate processes and participating entities, including of the provision of other levels of management, primarily asset or asset portfolio management - Asset / Portfolio.

2

Asset / Portfolio Management

The service is subordinate to fund/capital management. The fund's strategy and business plan (another capital-related mechanism) include:

1

Strategic Planning
  • Asset/portfolio strategy as the basis and key principles of the Asset Plan, its formation and implementation.
  • Rental strategy - the strategy of marketing the object, renting and selecting agents.

2

Finance
  • The asset plan and its budget - the development of documents, monitoring its relevance and auditing if necessary.
  • Transactions are the organization of asset purchases when investing capital and selling them when you exit an investment.
  • Cash balance management, including liquidity management, implementation and payment control.

3

Reporting and evaluation
  • Reporting, including regular financial and KPI reports on assets.
  • Accounting and formation of regulatory reporting at the asset level.
  • Valuation real estate for accounting, tax, credit purposes.

4

Organization and Control
  • Tender and monitoring of all external service providers related to transactions and asset ownership.
  • Monitoring and regular physical asset inspections.

  • Real estate insurance organization.

  • Management of claims and claims for all types of claims and penalties.

5

Compliance and Data Management
  • Asset-level compliance to ensure compliance with all asset regulations and procedures.
  • Asset data management, including registration, storage and secure protection.

6

Management
  • Project management - construction management, development and repair.
  • The organization of commercial property management - Property Management.

3

Property Management

The service is subordinate to asset management. The property manager's complex of functions includes:

1

Administration
  • Rent management, including the collection and accounting of rent and other payments, daily relationships with tenants.
  • Administer service fees.
  • Tax administration, admission and payment.
  • Timing management and lease extension.

2

Relationships with tenants
  • Promoting an asset to attract tenants and consumers.
  • Control of tenant redevelopment.
  • Control of tenant guarantees.
  • Confirmation of bills to be paid.

3

Finance
  • Budgeting for NOI calculation.
  • Insurance damages settlement

4

Safety
  • Maintaining the building's technical documents and compliance with regulatory requirements.
  • Control of all types of building security, control of the performers.
  • Checks of planned maintenance of the building, internal supervision.
  • Certification of all types of building safety, including fire and sanitary.

5

Management
  • Maintain a database (data room) of the building.
  • Maintaining the proper condition of the building and its — Facility management.
